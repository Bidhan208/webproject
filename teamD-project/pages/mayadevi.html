The Story of Maya Devi Temple: A Journey Through Time

The Maya Devi Temple in Lumbini, Nepal, is one of the most sacred sites in Buddhism, marking the exact place where Queen Maya Devi gave birth to Siddhartha Gautama, who later became Buddha.
